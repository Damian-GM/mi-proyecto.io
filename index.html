    ABOUT

    Karmalogic is an Argentine musician born in 1986 in the city of Castelar, Buenos Aires. He started in music at 15 years old through the guitar, and for 10 years he composed several albums and EP's of different genres within Rock and Metal, presenting them live by the Federal Capital and the inside from the province of Buenos Aires.


    During 2017, having already left his different musical projects aside, he met a new world within the Psychedelic Trance.


    Currently working on several of his own productions and collaborations with other national artists, he is on the search for his own sound, which combines emotional and groovy melodies with powerful basses and sounds characteristic of Psychedelic Trance.

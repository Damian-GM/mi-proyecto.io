    ABOUT

    ME

    My name es Damian, aka Karmalogic, and I´m an Argentine Dj & electronic music producer. I started in music at 15 years old and for many years I composed several albums and EP's of different genres within psychedelic rock, performing across South America and Europe.


    During 2012, having already left my different musical projects aside, I met a new world within the Psychedelic Trance.


    I release my first track in 2015 and since released docens more on labels such as Iono Music, Iboga Records, Maharetta Records, Sacred Technology and more. In 2020 I start Karmademy, an online music production academy, to help producers who love psytrance to achieve a good, clean and powerful sound.
